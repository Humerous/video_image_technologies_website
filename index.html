ABOUT - DAVID MILLER

David Miller was born and bred in Cape Town, South Africa and completed a Diploma in Sound Engineering after completing his initial schooling. He started working in the Film & Media industry in 2009. His technical expertise and interest helped him to become a seasoned Video Takeoff Operator and Digital Image Technician. The fact that David had a background in DIT and a passion for electronics made him eager to get more involved in the industry and in 2016 he founded Video Image Technologies.

Working with the world's top Directors of Photography, including Anthony Dod Mantle, David has made a great name for himself as a trustworthy technician. He is skilled in advanced video assistance, during which he works with multiple camera set-ups. He is also highly skilled as a QTake Operator, Digital Image Technician, Video Take-Off Operator, DIT/VT, Data Wrangler, VFX Wrangler and Streaming Operator. He has successfully completed projects for clients such as Coca-Cola, Hitachi, Sony, Samsung, Toyota Japan, Chevrolet and many others. He is a creative digital expert and is passionate about finding more innovative ways to merge film and audio, utilising the skills and experience he has built over the years.

When he is out of the office, he spends most of his working on future business endeavors and studying program development. David's sights are set on Video Image Technologies becoming a recognizable brand and expanding his services on offer down the line.
