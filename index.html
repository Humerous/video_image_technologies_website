Who We Are

Video Image Technologies is a cutting-edge video assist company operated and managed by David Miller. He has extensive experience using HD video and providing video and data wrangling services within the South African entertainment industry. We have the latest top-end equipment and the ability to customize our system for any production requirements.

It seems that most video assist companies use consumer monitors to play back video. At Video Image Technologies, we use professional HD monitors with built-in capabilities such as color correction, waveforms, scopes, and adjustable control panels to meet your specific needs.

“We are extremely professional and only use international standard equipment for all our jobs. That's the reason why all our clients keep coming back.”

Summary - David Miller

David Miller started working in the industry with Scarab Industries 7 years ago. Over time, David's savvy technical knowledge and interests got him to become a seasoned VT Operator and Data wrangler in the industry. David managed to carve a great profile for himself as a trustworthy technician, working for the world's top DOP and directors, including Anthony Dod Mantle.

He continues to enjoy these experiences and recently worked with one of Japan's top Directors, Hideyuki Tanaka. David's data management background and love for pushing buttons got him excited about the industry, leading him to start "Video Image Technologies."

When he's out of the office and offset, he spends most of his time producing music and performing under the name "Humerous," a well-known name locally and abroad. David's sights are set on becoming a recognizable brand with growing services to offer down the line. He studied sound engineering at "Cape Audio College" and has excelled in this space as well.

He is a creative digital expert and is passionate about finding better ways to merge video and sound, utilizing his skill and experience built over the years.

He is skilled in advanced video assist, for which he has to work with multiple camera setups, and he has 4 years of experience with Q-take. He has worked successfully for clients such as Coca Cola, Hitachi, Sony, Samsung, Toyota Japan, Chevrolet, and many others.
